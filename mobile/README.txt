VS Trade Simulator
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Introduction
-----------
VS Trade Simulator v1.0.1 is an application witch tries du calculate 
the best trade you can do beetwen many locations such as Planets, 
Installations or Vessel in the game VegaStrike :
